Welcome to Jönköping!

What can you do here?

Jönköping is known for its beautiful lakes, the fantastic student life and their top rated restaurants. If you visit Jönköping in the summer, you have to stop by Vätterstranden or have an ice cream at the pier in the center of town.

What are good places to go out?

  • Akademien - (for students only!)
  • Twinn - bar and restaurant!
  • Glädje - good dancefloor!

What are good places to eat?
